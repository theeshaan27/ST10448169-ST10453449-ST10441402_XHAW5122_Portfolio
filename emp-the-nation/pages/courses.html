Explore Our Training Programs

We offer a variety of training programs to help individuals enhance their skills and become more competitive in the job market. Below is a list of our most popular courses:

Six-Month Training Programs

  • First Aid - Learn life-saving techniques such as CPR, treating burns and wounds, and emergency management.
  • Sewing - Master the art of sewing, including garment repair, pattern making, and designing clothes.
  • Landscaping - Understand the principles of garden design, plant selection, and maintenance for stunning landscapes.
  • Life Skills - Develop essential skills such as communication, time management, and problem-solving for professional success.

Short Six-Week Courses

  • Child Minding - Gain practical knowledge in child care, including child development, safety, and activities.
  • Cooking - Learn the basics of cooking healthy, delicious meals with an emphasis on nutrition and hygiene.
  • Garden Maintenance - Discover the skills needed to maintain a beautiful garden, including lawn care, pruning, and pest control.
